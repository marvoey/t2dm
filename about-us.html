About Us

T2DM is a Virginia LLC that provides civil engineering consulting services primarily in the Northern Virginia area.

T2DM was incorporated in 2011 and has continued to build a reputation as a responsive and professional engineering services company. The objective of T2DM is to meet client needs with regard to design, budget and schedule, while maintaining the highest project quality and constructability.

T2DM provides professional engineering services primarily to the private sector, specializing in site engineering for residential, retail commercial building site plans, industrial, and municipal types of projects. Some specific engineering services include hydrologic design and analysis, roadway traffic flow analysis, and site plans of any type or application.

A partial list services includes the following:

  • feasibility studies and preliminary plans
  • subdivision plans and grading plans
  • drainage and floodplain studies
  • site development plans
  • highway and street design
  • utility and pavement system design
  • erosion and sedimentation control
  • storm water and BMP system design
  • permit application processing
  • hydraulic / hydrologic studies
  • internet technology services
  • value engineering
