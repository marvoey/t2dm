Projects

These are just a sample of some of the projects and the services provided by T2DM.

Loudoun Valley Estates

Culvert extension for right turn lane and floodplain study using HEC-RAS model for the proposed conditions according to Virginia Drainage Manual and the Scour Analysis Report is prepared to confirm the recommendations on foundation bearing levels provided from Geotechnical Investigation and submitted to Virginia Department of Transportation.

Lena Mill

Flexible pavement design for the subdivision and secondary roads to meet minimum requirements of VDOT. Develop construction plans with necessary applications to Fairfax county.

Twin Lakes

Using Civil 3D, developed the mass grading plan for 82 town homes for the Twin Lakes community, a 34.5 acre site in Fredrick County, VA allowing for accurate earthwork takeoffs. Civil 3D corridor and grading objects allowed for "what-if" analysis to help client analyze and determine grading options in the mountainous terrain.
